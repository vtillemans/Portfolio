Farm Fresh Produce

I designed and built the About Page using HTML and CSS (LESS). I also wrote the content under the About Us heading, and made the top image. I made three responsive columns using a property that had not been taught to us by this point. We learned to use FlexBox but I wanted to find an easier way, so I researched and learned about using WebKit!

This way, I could put a `style` tag in the Head of the HTML file. When we had our team meeting at the end of the project, my group was impressed and asked me how I was able to do that. So I taught them how to use WebKit too!

Ballet Wichita

Ballet Wichita, the largest nonprofit ballet organization in Kansas, was using a Facebook Page as their website when I met them. I led the team that built them a feature-rich and robust website using WordPress. I built custom forms that revoluionized their audition process - changing a standard that had been in place for over 40 years.

I also installed software that made it possible to offer classes to their members, something they had never done. Their beautiful, powerful website led to a significant increase in sales and corporate donations.

Her beautiful new website earned her national attenion. She had producers from Miami and New York contact her for a commerical and reality show. In addition, her website remains in the top position in Google's search results.
